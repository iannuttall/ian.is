This is the first post on the new ian.is. The site runs on Astro with content
collections, deployed to Cloudflare Workers. Most pages are static, but Workers
give me room to add server-side features later without moving hosts.

## Why content collections

Posts are plain Markdown files with typed frontmatter. That keeps the writing
portable and lets me build a small desktop app to edit them locally without
fighting a database.

## What's next

- Free tools for SEO and AI
- A proper newsletter over on [list.ian.is](https://list.ian.is)
- Interactive posts backed by D1 and R2 where it makes sense
