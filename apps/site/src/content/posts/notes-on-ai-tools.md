Most useful AI tools are small. They take one messy input, do one transformation
a person would rather not do by hand, and hand back something you can use in
under a second.

That's the bar I want to hold the tools on this site to. No dashboards to learn,
no accounts to create for a thing you'll run twice a year.

## A rough test

Before building one, I ask:

1. Would I use this weekly?
2. Can it run without me babysitting it?
3. Does it save real minutes, not just feel clever?

If the answer to all three is yes, it's worth shipping.
